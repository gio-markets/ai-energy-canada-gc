1. The AI Energy Tsunami: An Unprecedented Demand Surge

The exponential growth of Artificial Intelligence is creating a voracious and rapidly accelerating demand for electricity. Data centers, the backbone of AI, are on track to consume a massive share of global power, pushing existing energy grids to their absolute limits and creating an urgent need for new, sustainable energy sources.

Projected Global Data Center Consumption (TWh)

Global data center electricity demand is projected to more than double between 2022 and 2026, reaching a level comparable to the entire nation of Japan.

AI's Share of US Electricity

By 2028, data centers could consume up to 12% of all electricity in the United States, a threefold increase from 2023 levels, largely driven by AI's expansion.
